The first step

Before drawing, we need to have a --- pencil. There are something you need to know:

  • Understanding the numbers and letters on the end of pencils.
    • It is called graphite scale.
      "B" stands for soft black, "H" stands for hard, "HB" stands for hard black, and "F" stands for firm.
      The numbers classify the degrees of the intensity.
  • Sharpen a pencil
    • For a beginner, you can just use a sharpener to start.
    • If you want to be more professional, try to use a utility knife.
      Hold the pencil with your non-dominant hand and hold the knife with the other hand firmly. Push the blade slowly with your thumb of your non-dominant hand
