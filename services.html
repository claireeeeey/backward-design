The first step

First, we need to have the posture and action of what you want to draw, either someone or something.

  • Capture the outline of the posture and action using simple straight lines.
    • It may be hard to imagine what it looks like, so it will be easier to use photos or real obkects as reference.
      In this step, you don't need to rush to draw the whole figure, we just need the general structure.
  • Define the form
    • For a beginner, you can just use a sharpener to start.
    • If you want to be more professional: try to use a utility knife.
      Hold the pencil with your non-dominant hand and hold the knife with the other hand firmly.
      Push the blade slowly with your thumb of your non-dominant hand and remove the wooden layers of the pencil lightly.
      The length of the exposed refills should be round half an inch.
    • Be carefull!
